About

My name is Magdalena Nowak. For over 4 years I have been working in one of the leading architectural offices in Poland. I have opportunity to work with large city projects, such as the Revitalisation of Park Pole Mokotowskie in Warsaw, Renovation with adaptation historic Theater the name of Stefan Żeromski in Kielcach or Project Museum History of Poland. I also participate in design conception, social housing projects and facilities from the tourism sector.

I work in full technical scale from urban planninng, housing and interior design to product design.

I use BIM technology in Revit software.

Summation

I am interested about health and enviroment friendly architecture and construction. I am trying to expand my knowledge in the area of healthy building materials. I appreciate designing architecture with surrounding landscape, creating friendly enviroment for customers. I believe that the coherent combination of architecture and greenery is an elevating value the quality of life of both residents of the designed facilities and the entire city.
